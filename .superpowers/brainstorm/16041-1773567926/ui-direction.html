Which TARDIS console aesthetic should we build toward?

A) Classic Console

Dark navy/blue palette. Glowing cyan accents. Circular buttons and dials reminiscent of the TARDIS console room. Clean, sci-fi feel. Think modern 10th/11th Doctor era console.

B) Time Vortex

Deep purple/orange palette. Swirling vortex background with particle effects. More dramatic and immersive. Inspired by the opening credits aesthetic. Animated energy patterns.

C) Gallifreyan Minimal

Dark background with gold/amber accents. Circular Gallifreyan-inspired decorative elements. Elegant and mysterious. Sharp corners, minimal chrome. Feels ancient and advanced.
